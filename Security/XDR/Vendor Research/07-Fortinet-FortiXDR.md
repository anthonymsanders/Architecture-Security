| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Product Name** | FortiXDR (part of Fortinet Security Fabric) |

## Key Capabilities

- **Security Fabric Integration**: Tight correlation across all Fortinet products
- **FortiAI**: AI-driven automated investigation and response
- **FortiSOAR**: Built-in SOAR for playbook automation
- **FortiSIEM**: Converged SIEM for log management and compliance
- **Network-first detection**: Deep network visibility via FortiGate firewalls
- **FortiGuard Labs**: Threat intelligence from FortiGuard research team

## Strengths

- Tight Security Fabric integration across the full Fortinet portfolio
- Strong network detection via FortiGate NGFW (largest NGFW market share)
- Cost-effective for SMB and mid-market
- FortiSOAR for automation
- FortiSIEM for SIEM convergence
- Flexible deployment (cloud, on-prem, hybrid)
- Native email security (FortiMail)

## Weaknesses

- Best value only in Fortinet-only environments
- Endpoint detection (FortiEDR) less mature than CrowdStrike, SentinelOne
- Limited third-party ecosystem compared to open XDR platforms
- Gartner rates as niche player for endpoint protection
- Less advanced AI/ML capabilities than top-tier vendors
- Market perception lags behind CrowdStrike, Microsoft, Palo Alto

## Ideal For

- Fortinet firewall customers wanting integrated XDR
- SMB and mid-market organizations seeking cost-effective security
- Environments wanting on-premises or hybrid deployment
- Organizations wanting a single-vendor security fabric approach
